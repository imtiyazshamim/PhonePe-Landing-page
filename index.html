engineering tech change

At PhonePe, we strongly believe in ‘Positive Disruption’ — in building technology that helps all our stakeholders positively, by increasing the size of the pie for everyone in our operating ecosystem. We believe in sharing what we have learnt in our journey. This is a key step towards our vision of creating financial inclusion for a Billion Indians, and that is the reason we launched ‘etc’ — engineering tech change!

As part of ‘etc’, here is a unique initiative to bring together Women in Tech & Product roles across industries. This series of events is designed to openly share Tech learnings from PhonePe, with women aspiring to build their careers in the growing FinTech ecosystem.

Theme - ‘Scale’ and its relevance today

One of the primary reasons why PhonePe is able to handle billions of transactions per month is because we strongly believe in ‘Simplicity builds scalability’. All our engineering systems were built for scale. Our Tech team spends considerable time and energy in database sharding and load communication to reduce our failure rate.

We also scaled our infrastructure components that provided observability, alerting and automated algorithmic corrective measures. This helped in building our engineering ecosystem to scale seamlessly with exponential growth. ‘etc’ interactions will provide a rare insight into what makes the systems at PhonePe work seamlessly to power millions of transactions and make lives better for the people of India.

Partnering with AnitaB

PhonePe has partnered with AnitaB.org, in our mission to increase the representation of women technologists at all levels. This event aims at facilitating engagement and building a network across the Women in STEM and non-binary communities across India.

This event is an excellent opportunity to interact with the Local Communities, Prominent Tech Leaders, PhonePe leaders and the AnitaB.org team.

If you’re a Woman in Tech, here is why you should attend this event:

‘etc’ is an opportunity to interact with leaders from the tech world and ask intriguing questions.

Upskill yourself in one of the core and trending topics in the engineering space - Database Scaling.

Learn about solving a problem from different engineering and product perspectives.

Get inspired through your interaction with senior leaders in the tech industry.

About PhonePe

PhonePe was founded in December 2015, and has emerged as India’s largest payments app, enabling digital inclusion for consumers and merchants alike. With 40+ crore (400+ Million) registered users, one in four Indians are now on PhonePe. The company has also successfully digitized 3.2 crore (32 Million) offline merchants spread across Tier 2,3,4 and beyond, covering 99% pin codes in the country. PhonePe forayed into financial services in 2017, providing users with a safe and convenient option to buy 24-karat gold, and has recently also launched silver on its platform. Since then, PhonePe has introduced several Mutual Funds and Insurance products like tax-saving funds, liquid funds, international travel insurance, life insurance, COVID-19 insurance amongst others. Using PhonePe, users can also send and receive money, recharge mobile, DTH, pay at stores and make all their utility payments. PhonePe was recently recognized as the Most Trusted Brand for Digital Payments as per the Brand Trust Report 2022 by Trust Research Advisory (TRA).

About AnitaB

AnitaB.org India currently connects with local communities in Indian cities such as Delhi (NCR), Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Mumbai. They are referred to as chapters and are involved in developing relations, knowledge, student communities, and a support system in their local communities. Their mission is to inspire, encourage, and help women technologists connect with each other and organizations nearby — allowing them to learn, exchange stories, and grow their careers.

Rahul is the co-founder and CTO at PhonePe. He is a serial entrepreneur in the consumer internet space and has built PhonePe into one of India’s fastest growing and largest fintech companies. He comes with over two decades of experience in consumer internet and enterprise software space. He has strong experience in building world-class tech platforms for web-scale, continuously scaling systems to deal with hyper-growth and evolving the processes and practices to meet the demands of a fast-growing organization. Prior to PhonePe he was working as the VP Engineering at Flipkart and was responsible for building the best-in-class supply chain system for e-commerce. He joined Flipkart in 2011 through the acquisition of Mallers Inc where he served as the Chief Technology Officer and built Mime360, their flagship product. Prior to Mallers, Rahul was with the Data Center Business Unit at Cisco Systems where he was part of the team that developed the market changing MDS 9000 family of SAN switches. He is named on multiple storage virtualization related patents. Rahul holds a Masters degree in Computer Science from Purdue University, USA and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Bombay (Mumbai) University, India (Gold Medalist).

Kanika Khetawat is a software developer at PhonePe and has been part of the organisation since its early days. Being a part of the payments team, She has been fortunate enough to experience the massive growth of the transactions and the tech challenges along with it. This has given her opportunities to work on complex problems dealing with scalability and optimisations, one of them being the database scalability to support the increasing storage requirements and operations. She has a master’s degree in computing systems from Georgia Tech and a Bachelor’s degree from NIT Allahabad.
